SreeTech™ combines a series of technology disruptions that together provide an unprecedented service in the marketplace.  A few ...

Biometric security, authentication and identification at the forefront of world standards is fully integrated into the SreeTech™ Operating System.

Breakthrough Semantics that eliminate the need for coding and uses natural language to initiate queries. All data BIG and small, can be interrogated, analysed and engineered to provide immediate answers.

Blockchained Transactions.  SreeTech™ delivers a blockchained plug-play solution to the rapidly expanding requirements of government, cities, large enterprise and SME businesses incubation.

From Biometric Technologies to Self-Service Machines (Kiosks and more) to Machine Learning based solutions, get going from day one with our pluggable and highly scalable services. They are designed to seamlessly integrate into your existing Folio (Mobile Apps, Enterprise Apps) and let you exploit all the business advantages of cutting-edge technologies. 

SELF-SERVICE MACHINES/KIOSKS

Fed-up with old fashion, ineffectual and complicated kiosks? We are a leading provider of interactive touchscreen kiosks, with built-in multi-factor face, finger-print and other biometric authentications.

BEACONS/RFID BASED SOLUTIONS

Whether you want to track high value assets in a large assembly or exhibition area or manage their productivity in a hospital, we have ready-to-use tagging solutions.      

DIGITAL SIGNAGE MANAGEMENT

Communication today is about visualization and live data. Our Digital signage with built-in camera is a smart visual communication solution for businesses to measure audience engagement.

 

DEDUPE ANALYSIS

Our dedupe analysis engines capture duplicate bank customers or telecom subscribers and more. They are also plug-and-play and offer real-time analysis as well. Talk to us to see how this technology can help you.

BIOMETRIC WEIGHMENTS

Fed-up with old, ineffectual and complicated Weighment system? We are a leading provider of Biometric Weighment solutions, with built-in face and finger print authentications.

HAND-HELD DEVICES

Want to take your business to customer door steps? We are the provider of Hand-held devices with built-in face, finger print and other biometric authentications.

 

BIOMETRIC AUTH, VERIFICATION & VALIDATION

Our Biometric solutions can be deployed in many contexts. From tracking Wanted lists to identifying your customers to attendance management to access control and more. Talk to us to find out how this contact-less technology can do wonders for your business.  

INDUSTRY 4.0 IOT

Enable remote monitoring, analyze trends, or save gillions? We help you to explore where your devices seamlessly interact with each other to gather, store, and process data. You may boost your productivity, generate new revenue streams, enhance security or radically transform your service support - all of this becomes possible with IoT.

AI & COGNITIVE ANALYTICS

From Machine Learning to Natural Language Processing, our plug-and-play technology services in this area can propel you ahead of your competition. Talk to us.
